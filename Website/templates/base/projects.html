Tracker

With the framework Scrapy I created a program that has the ability to scrape special internet sites for prices. Using the Cron-Daemon I frequently scraped the prices of chosen Items and saved them into a SQL-Database. As soon as there was a drop in more than 20% there was an Email sent to my account notifying me about the new prices.
